GELD Jewelry Studio — Full UX/UI Design Brief & Structure for Google Stitch
--------------------------------------------------------------
Overall Style Guide:
- Visual Mood: Premium, minimalistic, elegant, calm confidence. No visual noise.
- Typography: Playfair Display (headers, serif) + Inter (body, sans-serif).
- Color Palette:
  - Background: #FAF8F5 (warm white)
  - Text: #1A1A1A (deep black)
  - Accent: #B8945A (muted gold)
  - Borders/Lines: #E8E4DF (warm gray)
  - Secondary BG: #F3F0EC (light beige)
- UX Principles: Clean composition, clear hierarchy, fast path to catalog. Mobile-first adaptivity.

--------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 1: HOME PAGE (Homepage)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Structure:
1. HEADER: Fixed top bar (sticky). Logo "GELD" left. Nav links (Rings, Earrings, Pendants, Necklaces, Bracelets, Collections). Right side: "Catalog" button, Search icon, Favorites icon, Cart icon. Mobile: Burger menu icon.
2. HERO BLOCK: 100vh height. Background: Full-width high-end jewelry image. Center/Left text overlay: "Laboratory Diamonds" (Heading 1), "GELD jewelry studio" (Subtitle), Two buttons: [View Collection] (Dark) [About the Brand] (Transparent/Ghost).
3. CATEGORIES BLOCK: Title: "Catalog". Grid of 5 category cards (Rings, Earrings, Pendants, Necklaces, Bracelets). Visual placeholders (squares with neutral gradients). Links to catalog.
4. SUBCATEGORIES BLOCK: Section title: "Rings". Sub-grid of 5 types (Engagement, Wedding, With Diamonds, With Sapphires, Without Stones). Each as a visual card.
5. COLLECTIONS BLOCK: Section title: "Collections". Grid of 4 collection cards (Essence, Lumière, Éternité, Rivière). Each card: Large image + collection name + short description.
6. ABOUT BRAND BLOCK: Split layout. Left: Image of the brand/founder. Right: Tag "About the Brand", Heading "30 Years of Experience. One Brand.", Short text description, Button [Learn More].
7. USPs BLOCK (Advantages): Section title hidden. 4-column grid: [Certificate] Icon + text, [Engraving] Icon + text, [SPA Care] Icon + text, [Gift Cards] Icon + text.
8. EXPERTISE BLOCK: Dark background (#1A1A1A). Left text: "World of GELD", Heading "Knowledge is the Basis of Trust". 3 numbered vertical blocks (1. Lab Diamonds, 2. Natural Stones, 3. Personalization). Right: Image.
9. BLOG/JOURNAL (Optional): Section title: "Journal". Grid of 3 article cards (Advice, Trends, News) with image, tag, title, date.
10. FOOTER: Dark background. 5 columns: [Logo + Social Links], [Catalog (Rings, Earrings, etc.)], [Buyers Info (Delivery, Payment, Engraving, etc.)], [About the Brand (History, Contacts)] + [Contact Info (Phone, Email, Address, Hours)]. Bottom row: Copyright + USP icons (Certificate, Engraving, SPA).

--------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 2: CATALOG PAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------
Structure:
1. HEADER & BREADCRUMBS: Same as homepage. Breadcrumbs: Home / Catalog.
2. CATALOG HERO: Title "Catalog". Filter toggle button (mobile).
3. LAYOUT: 2-column grid. Left: Sidebar (Sticky). Right: Main content.
4. SIDEBAR FILTERS:
   - Categories: All, Rings, Earrings, Pendants, Necklaces, Bracelets.
   - Subcategories: Engagement, Wedding, With Diamonds, Without Stones.
   - Price: Input [From] - [To].
   - Material: Checklist (Gold 585, Gold 750, Silver, Platinum).
   - Stones: Checklist (Diamonds, Sapphires, Rubies, Emeralds, Lab Diamonds).
5. MAIN CONTENT:
   A. FEATURED PRODUCT: Large split block (left large image, right product name, collection, price, button [View]). Mark as "New".
   B. PRODUCT GRID: 4 columns. Each card: Image (with hover effect showing second view), Product Name, Price.
   C. POPULAR SECTION: Title "Popular". Grid of 4 larger cards.
   D. LOAD MORE: Centered button [Show More].

--------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE 3: PRODUCT CARD PAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------
Structure:
1. HEADER & BREADCRUMBS: Home / Catalog / Rings / Product Name.
2. SPLIT LAYOUT: Left Gallery, Right Info.
3. GALLERY: Large main image area with "Every diamond certified" badge. Below: 5 small thumbnails for switching images.
4. PRODUCT INFO:
   - Tag: "Essence" (Collection name).
   - Title: "Ring with Diamond".
   - SKU: "Article: G-2501".
   - Price: "185 000 ₽" (Large font). "Including VAT" (small).
5. VARIATIONS (Carat):
   - Title: "Carat Weight (Optional)".
   - 3 Buttons: "0.3 ct — 120 000 ₽", "0.5 ct — 185 000 ₽", "1.0 ct — 340 000 ₽".
6. SIZE SELECTOR:
   - Title: "Size".
   - Row of circular/rounded buttons: [15] [15.5] [16] [16.5] [17] [17.5] [18] [18.5] [19].
7. CTAs:
   - [Add to Cart] (Dark button with cart icon).
   - [Order] (Light button).
   - Favorite icon (Heart).
8. USP SECTION (Under CTAs):
   - [Certificate icon] Certified stones.
   - [Gold icon] Free Engraving.
   - [Heart icon] Free SPA Care.
9. DESCRIPTION & SPECS:
   - Description text.
   - Characteristics table: Material (White Gold 585), Stone (Diamond 0.5 ct VVS1 G), Cut (Round Perfect), Certification (GIA), Weight (3.2g).
10. RECOMMENDATIONS: Title "Looked at with this". Grid of 4 product cards.
11. VIEWED EARLIER: Title "Viewed Earlier". Grid of 2 product cards.
12. BACK TO CATALOG: Link with back arrow at bottom of page.

--------------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL UX/COMPOSITION NOTES:
- Grid system: 12 columns on desktop (1280px max-width). 24px gutters.
- Spacing: Generous whitespace (80px sections). Airy but grounded.
- Visuals: Warm neutral gradients for placeholders. High-end jewelry photography vibe.
- Mobile view: Single column stacks. Sidebar becomes a slide-out drawer. Catalog grid -> 2 columns.
