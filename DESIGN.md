# GELD Jewelry Studio — Design & Visual Concept

> **Status:** Approved / Live
> **Style:** Premium Minimal (Lavivion-inspired, strict geometry, high-end e-commerce)
> **Language:** Russian everywhere
> **Logo:** GELD (All caps)

This document describes the design concept for the GELD website. It serves as the absolute authority for generating future iterations, layouts, or designs.

## 1. Visual Style Guide

### 1.1 Mood & Atmosphere
*   **Vibe:** Premium, strict, confident, airy, clean.
*   **Inspiration:** La Vivion. The focus is on high-quality photography, negative space (whitespace), and sharp, structured grids.
*   **Visual Noise:** Zero gradients. Zero shadows. No decorative borders.

### 1.2 Color Palette
Strictly black-and-white with a subtle gold accent for active states only.

| Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Background** | `#FFFFFF` | Pure white background everywhere. |
| **Primary Text** | `#111111` | Deep black for titles and prices. |
| **Secondary Text** | `#666666` | Medium gray for descriptions/dates. |
| **Border Lines** | `#E0E0E0` | Subtle divider lines and borders. |
| **Hover/Active** | `#B8945A` | Muted gold (used ONLY for buttons, hover effects, or the 'active' filter state). |
| **Success/Valid** | `#008000` | Standard green for form success/error. |

### 1.3 Typography
*   **Font Family:** Strict geometric sans-serif. Use **SF Pro Display** (Mac), **Inter** (Web), or **Roboto**. No serif fonts (no Playfair, no Times, no Georgia) for UI elements.
*   **Weights:** Light (`300`) or Regular (`400`) for body text. Bold (`600` or `700`) only for headers.

| Element | Size (px) | Weight | Styling |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **H1 (Page Title)** | 48px | 400 (Regular) | All-caps, wide tracking (letter-spacing 0.05em). |
| **H2 (Section)** | 36px | 400 (Regular) | Underlined with a 1px black line. |
| **H3 (Category Name)** | 18px | 600 (Bold) | Centered below images. |
| **Body** | 16px | 400 | Line-height 1.6. |
| **Small Text** | 12px | 400 | Used for specs, tags, dates. |

## 2. Layout & Grid
*   **Grid System:** 12-column layout on Desktop. 24px gutters between columns.
*   **Max-Width:** 1400px.
*   **Container Padding:** 60px left/right.
*   **Mobile Layout:** All grids collapse to 2 columns. Sidebar (filters) becomes a horizontal slider or a drawer toggle.

---

## 3. Page Structure

### 3.1 Global Header
*   **Top Bar:** Thin (30px), background `#FFFFFF`. Left: "+7 (930) 030-50-02". Right: "Delivery", "About", "Contacts".
*   **Main Bar:** White background. 
    *   *Logo:* **"GELD"** in strictly black, bold, sans-serif text.
    *   *Nav:* Centered links: "Rings", "Earrings", "Pendants", "Bracelets", "Necklaces", "Collections".
    *   *Right:* Search Icon | Favorites Icon | Cart | Login.

### 3.2 Homepage
1.  **Hero Slider:** Full-width (100% height of screen). High-end images of jewelry on models. Text overlay: Large serif-free "NEW COLLECTION" in white, with a small "View Collection" button.
2.  **Categories Grid:** 5 cards in a row. Each card: Image above, text "RINGS", "EARRINGS", etc., centered below with arrows. Border-bottom on hover.
3.  **New Collections:** 3 columns of full-height images or large product shots with text "AMORE", "CONTESSA", "KAMNI".
4.  **Advantages (USP):** 4 rows with thin icons (envelope, diamond, heart). Texts: "Certificate", "Engraving", "Personalization", "SPA Care".
5.  **Blog/Journal:** 3 vertical cards with article titles.

### 3.3 Catalog Page
1.  **Hero:** Simple white banner "CATALOG". Minimalist breadcrumbs.
2.  **Sidebar:** 
    *   White background, sticky. 
    *   Sections: Categories, Price (inputs), Materials (checkboxes), Stones (checkboxes).
3.  **Product Grid:** 4 columns. Large images with hover effect (showing back view or model view).
    *   Under card: Name (small, strict font), Metal/Stone specs (even smaller, gray).
4.  **Pagination:** Simple numbers centered or aligned right.

### 3.4 Product Page (Item Card)
1.  **Gallery:** 
    *   Left: Large vertical product image (white background). Side vertical thumbnails.
    *   Right: Information panel.
2.  **Info Panel (Sticky):**
    *   Title (e.g., "Ring 'Amore' with Diamond").
    *   Article number.
    *   Price (Massive, black font, e.g., 48px).
    *   **CTA:** Large black button "Add to Cart".
    *   **Variations:** Selection for size (buttons in a row) and carat (radio buttons).
    *   **Tabs:** Description | Characteristics.
3.  **"Looked At":** 4 horizontal product cards at the bottom.

## 4. Strict Constraints (DO NOT IGNORE)

*   **STOP:** Do not use serifs (Playfair Display) for text.
*   **STOP:** Do not use gradients for backgrounds; use pure `#FFFFFF`.
*   **STOP:** Do not translate "Catalog", "Add to Cart" or other UI elements into English. Everything must be in Russian.
*   **STOP:** The logo text **must** be strictly **"GELD"**, in black, uppercase. Never "Gel", "Geld", "GELd".
